What Can It Do?

Keep Your Timetable

It can be annoying to reopen SI-net everytime you need to check your timetable. Upload it to SyncUQ and never worry again.

Know Who's Free

Get notifed which of your friends are free when you start your break. Never eat lunch alone again!

Keep It Private

Sometimes you need some space. Turn on incognito mode and appear unavailable. It'll be our little secret.

SyncUQ Mobile

Logging onto sinet or uq timetable planner is a pain on mobile. On SyncUQ, your timetable is the first thing you see when you log into the app, making it easier to find out when and where your next class is.

Included in the app is 'Who's Free', a nifty feature that allows you to find out who of your close friends are on a break at uni.

We've also embedded all your upcoming assessment into 'What's Due?', so you can find out all your assessment for all your subjects instantly.

We've gone to great lengths to make SyncUQ the only app you need to manage your time at uni. So jump on now to stress less and have more fun :)

SyncUQ Desktop

Phone out of battery? Don't have a phone? No problem. SyncUQ has a desktop webapp that has all the same features as our mobile version but it's all just a little bit bigger. Find your friends, view your calendar, and find out your upcoming assessment easily on your laptop or desktop.

We reccomend you use the desktop app to import your calendar, it's a little bit easier and you can't modify UQ timetable planner on mobile.
